My History

I grew up in BC, in the city of Maple Ridge. After high school I went to Vancouver Film School for game design, then landed an 18 month gameplay design contract after I graduated! When my contract ended however the games industry took a turn for the worst, with thousands of layoffs and maany studio closures, making it challenging to find work. After a year and a half of portfolio work, side jobs and job applications I decided to pursue UX design at BCIT!
